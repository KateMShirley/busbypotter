Busby Potter is the boutique web design studio of developer Kate Busby and designer Eléna Potter.

We believe that a beautiful, effective website can establish your brand, convert visitors into customers, and ultimately grow your business.

Why Team Up?

As freelancers, we’re often approached by companies in need of a new website. After referring one another to projects, we decided it was time to make it official.

By teaming up, we’re able to offer start-to-finish websites, crafting captivating, accessible digital experiences. From the very beginning, we seamlessly blend the design and development processes to marry form and function, providing the best solution for your company’s unique needs.

Eléna Potter

Designer

Eléna is a hardwired optimist who believes in building approachable brands and having a lot of fun along the way. She works closely with clients to better understand their audience, using that information to elevate the brand and create meaningful experiences for the user. Eléna oversees creative direction on Busby Potter projects, coordinating a cohesive vision through design, imagery and content.

Kate Busby

Developer

Kate enjoys collaborating with clients to create engaging, interactive web experiences with projects that bridge the intersection of design and function. Here at Busby Potter, Kate is in charge of development and oversees all technical direction.

Design

Still got questions?
Are you an agency?

Definitely not, and we pride ourselves on that. We each had clients coming to us with website work, and would need to outsource a designer or developer. So, we banded together. Strength in numbers.Working with us is like hiring freelancers to do your work, but with the added benefit of us knowing just how the other person works. Which saves you time reviewing portfolios and onboarding and saves us time in learning how the other works.

How does communication work?

Just like any client–freelance relationship, we’ll each manage and communicate with you on our respective part of the project. However, being just two people, we work closely together throughout each and every project, making sure we’re both involved in 100% of the process.

Do you do both designed-from-scratch sites and redesigns?

Yes, we do!

I need more than just design & development. Do you have other capabilities?

If we don’t do it ourselves, we know someone who does. We have our own go-to freelancers for everything from copywriting to photography, content creation to strategy. We’re more than happy to make recommendations. Just ask!
